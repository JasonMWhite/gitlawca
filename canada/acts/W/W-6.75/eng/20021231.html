S.C. 2000, c. 29 # An Act to repeal An Act to incorporate the Western Canada Telephone Company (S.C. 2000, c. 29) Full Document: * [HTMLFull Document: An Act to repeal An Act to incorporate the Western Canada Telephone Company](FullText.html) | * [XMLFull Document: An Act to repeal An Act to incorporate the Western Canada Telephone Company](/eng/XML/W-6.75.xml) [3 KB] | * [PDFFull Document: An Act to repeal An Act to incorporate the Western Canada Telephone Company](/PDF/W-6.75.pdf) [124 KB] Act current to 2015-05-25 # An Act to repeal An Act to incorporate the Western Canada Telephone Company S.C. 2000, c. 29 Assented to 2000-10-20 An Act to repeal An Act to incorporate the Western Canada Telephone Company Her Majesty, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate and House of Commons of Canada, enacts as follows: ###### Marginal note:Repeal **1.** An Act to incorporate the Western Canada Telephone Company, being chapter 66 of the Statutes of Canada, 1916, is repealed. ###### Marginal note:Legal existence continues **2.** TELUS Communications (B.C.) Inc., incorporated under the Act referred to in section 1, as amended, and continued under the [Canada Business Corporations Act](/eng/acts/C-44), continues to exist as a person in law despite the repeal and is subject to the [Canada Business Corporations Act](/eng/acts/C-44). ###### Marginal note:Rights not affected **3.** The rights of holders of securities, debentures under trust deeds and beneficiaries of other instruments issued by or in respect of TELUS Communications (B.C.) Inc. are not affected by the repeal. ###### Marginal note:Coming into force **Footnote *4.** This Act comes into force on a day to be fixed by order of the Governor in Council. * Return to footnote
